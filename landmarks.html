Qasr Al Hosn
Qasr Al Hosn has played a central role in Abu Dhabi's history, serving as a residence for the ruling family, a government headquarters, a council for consultation, and a national archive. Today, it stands as a symbol of the nation's heritage, serving as a living memorial and storyteller of Abu Dhabi's rich history.
