  • Abu Dhabi Airlines and Airports

    • Abu Dhabi is the capital city of the United Arab Emirates and is served by two major airports: Abu Dhabi International Airport and Al Ain International Airport. Abu Dhabi International Airport is one of the largest airports in the Middle East and serves as a hub for Etihad Airways, the national airline of the UAE. Etihad Airways operates flights to destinations around the world and is known for its high-end in-flight amenities and services. Al Ain International Airport primarily serves the city of Al Ain and its surrounding areas. Both airports have modern facilities and offer a range of services and amenities to passengers.
